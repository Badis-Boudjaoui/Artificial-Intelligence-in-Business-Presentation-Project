This project is a complete presentation about how Artificial Intelligence is transforming the business world. It covers the definition of AI, its main types, and how it helps companies improve efficiency, decision-making, and customer experience.

The presentation also includes real-world examples such as AI chatbots, recommendation systems (Amazon/Netflix), self-driving technologies (Tesla), logistics optimization, and AI applications in healthcare.

Finally, it explores the advantages, challenges, and the future of AI in modern businesses, highlighting key points like automation, data needs, privacy concerns, and the expected evolution of AI in organizations.
